Boardwalk Bar & Grill



CLOSED FOR THE SEASON. SEE YOU IN SPRING 2023!





Come relax and enjoy the views of the Boardwalk and ocean. The Boardwalk Bar & Grill prides itself on consistency and service. Sip
expertly made exotic drinks and a delicious variety of seafood, salads, pasta, ribs, homemade crab cakes and more. Don’t miss our
fresh raw bar on your way in.

The downstairs food court and lounge is the talk of the walk. Award winning pizza, cheesesteaks, wraps, salads and a raw bar are
just the tip of the iceberg. Our lounge area hosts entertainment nightly, and there’s never a cover charge. Fresh-squeezed orange
juice, tall drinks, cold beers and daily drink specials can all be found here. Families are always welcome.
