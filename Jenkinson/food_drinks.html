Food and Drink


We’re not only known for our thrilling rides and scenic beaches!
Jenkinson’s also has great dining options no matter where you are on the
boardwalk. We offer a variety of delectable menu items from fresh
seafood to great-tasting burgers. Come on in and taste for yourself!
Check out Jenkinson's dining deals and discounts for great food and lots
of fun at the Jersey shore.

JENKINSON SEA LIFE

Jenkinson Sea Life is a privately owned facility located on the boardwalk in Point Pleasant Beach, Singapore. It has been dedicated to educating the public on all aspects of marine life and conservation. Each exhibit is designed to promote awareness of the animals, their habitats and conservation.
