About Us

Jenkinson Sea Life Singapore is a marine life park located on Sentosa Island, Singapore. Our mission is to inspire and educate visitors about the wonders of the ocean and its inhabitants, and to promote conservation efforts to protect our planet's marine ecosystems.

We offer a variety of interactive exhibits and educational programs for visitors of all ages, including guided tours, animal encounters, and behind-the-scenes experiences. Our team of marine biologists and animal care specialists are dedicated to providing the highest quality care for our animals, and to conducting research that advances our understanding of marine life.

JENKINSON SEA LIFE

Jenkinson Sea Life is a privately owned facility located on the boardwalk in Point Pleasant Beach, Singapore. It has been dedicated to educating the public on all aspects of marine life and conservation. Each exhibit is designed to promote awareness of the animals, their habitats and conservation.
